Character Information

AB:A Sans

Sans, AKA "The Easiest Enemy" is the left character you can play under the "Undertale" section. He has 3 attack modes. He will dodge all attacks thrown at him as long as he still has his dodge bar.

AB:A Asgore

Asgore, AKA 'The King of the Underground' is the second character in the 'Undertale' character section. He only has 2 sets of attacks, Tridents and Fire. Asgore is classified as a 'Tank' He has 60 attack, 240 health, and 240 defense, making him the tankiest in the game so far. He does less damage than other characters but is made up for with his high stock of HP and defense. Hard combos are not recommended for new players.
