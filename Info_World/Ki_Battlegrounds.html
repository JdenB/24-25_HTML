Character Information

Earths Warrior

Earths Warrior is a close to mid-range character, who mainly relies on close range moves and m1s(basic punches). They are based off the character "Goku" from the anime "Dragon ball Z". His ultimate form is super Sayain, in which he has 2 close rannge combo moves and a counter.
